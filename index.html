Boiling Point is one of my favorite foods, it is a taiwanese hot pot which consist of many delicous ingredients. You could pick between any configuration you want, consisting of meats, vegetables, etc.

Poke bowls are delicious. It consists of various types of fish with different sauce combinations.

Instant noodles are the upmost favorite when it comes to portability and speed. They are delicious while retaining an "anytime" name.

There isn't a good meal without a good drink. Boba is the perfect drink with its combination of a regular drink(milk) and boba. Boba in its regular form is usually a small chewy ball that is quite sweet.
